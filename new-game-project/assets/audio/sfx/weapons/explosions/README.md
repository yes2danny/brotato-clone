# explosions/

Sounds for explosive projectile impacts and area-of-effect blasts.

Examples of what belongs here:
- RPG / rocket launcher impact
- Fireball landing / detonation
- Grenade explosion
- Any AoE skill that hits the ground with a boom

Keep these punchy and short — the impact should feel satisfying but not drown out other SFX.
Variations (small / medium / large) are welcome here.

## shot_muffled.wav — echo layer
Play this at very low volume (~10–20%) immediately after an explosion sound as a distant echo/rumble.
Gives the explosion a sense of physical weight and space — like the blast carried through the air.
Could also double as a muffled shotgun if a low-power shotgun weapon gets added later.
To use as echo: play explosion_quick.wav, then after ~0.1s play shot_muffled.wav at low volume.
